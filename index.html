My name is Michael Gragg, and I’m a visual designer. In August 2016, I earned a Bachelor's of Science Degree in Visual Communication Technology with a focus in Interactive Media at Bowling Green State University.

Since then, I’ve worked at the Precision Metalforming Association in Independence, Ohio as a web and graphic designer. On any given day you might find me designing graphics to promote an event, designing or coding our new website, creating social media imagery, or graphics for presentations, mailers and more.
